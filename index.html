About简介

Guokun Dai is an Associate Professor at Fudan University. His research focuses on subseasonal to seasonal predictability, Arctic influences on Eurasian extreme weather and climate, and AI-enabled methods for atmospheric science. 戴国锟，复旦大学副教授。研究主要关注次季节到季节尺度可预测性、北极对欧亚极端天气气候的影响，以及人工智能在大气科学中的应用。

He received his B.S. in Atmospheric Sciences from Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology in 2012, and his Ph.D. in Meteorology from the Institute of Atmospheric Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2017. 2012年毕业于南京信息工程大学，获理学学士学位；2017年毕业于中国科学院大气物理研究所，获理学博士学位。

He worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher and Associate Research Scientist at Fudan University, served as a Visiting Scientist at the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF), and has been Associate Professor at Fudan University since 2024. 曾任复旦大学博士后、青年副研究员，并于2023–2024年在欧洲中期天气预报中心担任访问学者，2024年起任复旦大学副教授。

Research Interests研究兴趣

Predictability可预测性

Predictability of mid-latitude weather and climate, with emphasis on extended-range and subseasonal forecasts.中纬度天气气候可预测性，重点关注延伸期和次季节尺度预报。

Arctic–Midlatitude Linkages北极—中纬度联系

Arctic sea-ice–air interactions and their impacts on Eurasian extreme cold events, Ural blocking, and NAO variability.北极海冰—大气相互作用及其对欧亚极端冷事件、乌拉尔阻塞和 NAO 变化的影响。

AI in Atmospheric Science人工智能气象应用

Applications of artificial intelligence and hybrid modeling to weather prediction, Earth system models, and ensemble forecasting.人工智能和混合建模在天气预报、地球系统模式和集合预报中的应用。
