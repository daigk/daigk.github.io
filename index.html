About简介

Guokun Dai is an Associate Professor at Fudan University. His research focuses on subseasonal to seasonal predictability, Arctic influences on Eurasian extreme weather and climate, and applications of artificial intelligence in atmospheric science.戴国锟，复旦大学副教授。研究主要关注次季节到季节尺度可预测性、北极对欧亚极端天气气候的影响，以及人工智能在大气科学中的应用。

Research Interests研究兴趣

Predictability可预测性

Predictability of mid-latitude weather and climate, with emphasis on extended-range and subseasonal forecasts.中纬度天气气候可预测性，重点关注延伸期和次季节尺度预报。

Arctic–Midlatitude Linkages北极—中纬度联系

Arctic sea-ice–air interactions and their impacts on Eurasian extreme cold events, Ural blocking, and NAO variability.北极海冰—大气相互作用及其对欧亚极端冷事件、乌拉尔阻塞和 NAO 变化的影响。

AI in Atmospheric Science人工智能气象应用

Applications of artificial intelligence and hybrid modeling to weather prediction, Earth system models, and ensemble forecasting.人工智能和混合建模在天气预报、地球系统模式和集合预报中的应用。
